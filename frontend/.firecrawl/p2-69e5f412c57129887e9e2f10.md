## Frontend Developer

[Limepack](https://thehub.io/startups/limepack) [Frederiksberg](https://thehub.io/jobs?location=Frederiksberg,%20Denmark) [Full-time](https://thehub.io/jobs?positionTypes=5b8e46b3853f039706b6ea70)

Salary
Competitive

#### **Do you enjoy turning complex problems into simple, intuitive user experiences?**

As a frontend developer at Limepack, you will take ownership of the user experience and improve it where it has the biggest impact across our platform.

If you like combining creative thinking with hands-on execution, and care about both the details and the bigger picture, keep reading.

#### **🌟 Our Mission**

Limepack is a fast-growing scale-up operating across 14 international markets. Our mission is to help food-to-go businesses stand out with branded takeaway packaging.

We aim to become our customers’ preferred partner, and that requires a world-class user experience.

#### **📋 Your responsibilities and impact**

You will take ownership of a core part of our webshop and play a key role in improving how customers experience Limepack.

Your job is not just to build features, but to make it easier for customers to find what they need and place orders without friction.

You will build, test, and ship improvements to the platform, and continuously refine what is already there.

You will work closely with Sales, Marketing, and our E-com Manager to understand where customers struggle and turn that into better solutions.

From idea to live feature, you will run projects end to end and keep improving them over time.

#### **Requirements**

**👉 Who you are**

- You care about making things simple and intuitive, even when the underlying problem is complex.

- You notice the details and are willing to spend the extra time to get things right.

- You think in flows, not just features, and make sure everything connects smoothly from start to finish.

- You naturally take ownership of your work and follow things through. You do not wait to be told what to fix, you spot it yourself.

- You enjoy working towards a bigger goal and hold yourself and Limepack’s UX to a high standard.


**🎓 Your Experience & Toolbox**

You have experience as a frontend developer where you had real ownership, either independently or as part of a team.

You are comfortable working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, and you use them to solve real problems, but not afraid of stepping outside your comfort zone, if that’s what it takes for crafting the best solution.

Experience with e-commerce is a plus.

You have worked with people outside of tech, such as sales or marketing, and know how to turn their input into actual improvements.

You are comfortable working independently, figuring things out, and moving projects forward without heavy structure.

English is our working language, so fluency is required.

**Our stack**

- **Frontend**: HTML, Sass (Bootstrap), Vanilla JavaScript

- **Design**: Figma, Excalidraw

- **Backend**: PHP (PrestaShop and Symfony)

- **Database**: MySQL

- **Infrastructure**: Git, Ubuntu, Docker, AWS S3


#### **Benefits**

**🚀 Who We Are and What We Offer**

Limepack is a Copenhagen-based company with a strong remote team. You will be joining an international team of 31 dedicated employees from all around the world. We firmly believe in a healthy work-life balance, a predictable work week, and that time off is completely off. We are a certified 'Happy Workplace' that regularly wins awards for fast growth - and we are just getting started! 🚀

We offer a 37-hour-a-week full-time position based in our Frederiksberg office. We value flexibility, offering a hybrid model where you can work from home up to 2 days a week.

**We offer:**

- Salary Range: 35.000 - 50.000 DKK depending on experience.

- Growth & Learning: We offer an education budget - just let us know what you need to grow your skills!

- Benefits: 6 weeks of paid time off, pay during sick days, and generous paid parental leave. Overtime is generally not expected, but you will be compensated should it occur.

- Culture: Challenging, meaningful work alongside 31 kick-ass and sweet colleagues from all around the world.


#### **📝 How to Apply**

Send your application, CV, and Cover Letter through this form.

The application deadline is the 30th of April, but we conduct interviews on a continuous basis, so we strongly encourage you to apply as soon as possible!

### Working at   Limepack

[See company profile](https://thehub.io/startups/limepack)

Limepack is a fast-growing European scale-up specializing in custom-printed takeaway packaging for brands in the HoReCa sector. We help restaurants, cafés, chains, and event businesses stand out with branded packaging such as paper cups, plastic cups, food boxes, bags, and other to-go packaging solutions.

Our ambition is to become Europe’s leading platform for printed takeaway packaging. We work closely with European producers and are committed to making branded packaging more accessible through free expert design support, low minimum order quantities, and fast, free shipping across Europe.

Limepack is a small but ambitious team that moves fast, works hard, and values a strong social culture. We eat lunch together, celebrate wins together, and enjoy a beer on Fridays after work. Our main office is located in Copenhagen at Frederiksberg.
