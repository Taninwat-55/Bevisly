#### **THE OPPORTUNITY**

Genta is an AI implementation partner for small and mid-sized companies. We help clients move to secure, measurable and operating AI agents.

We are looking for an AI Builder: a hands-on engineer and architect who can translate real business processes into production-grade AI agent systems. You will design the flow, build the automation, connect the data, implement guardrails, create observability and make sure the agent can be trusted in day-to-day use. This will be done with strong support from colleagues and strategic development house, supporting the build of of the agents.

**_The goal is simple: make AI concrete, safe and operational - with measurable ROI and humans in control._**

#### Role snapshot

**Company:** Genta

**Role:** AI Builder / Agentic Systems Engineer

**Focus:** Production-grade AI agents, workflow automation and data integrations

**Stack:** n8n, LangGraph / LangChain, TypeScript / Node.js, MCP, LLMs, PostgreSQL, Redis / BullMQ, APIs, Docker and EU-hosted infrastructure

**Location:** Copenhagen, Denmark

**Engagement:** Full Time

**Reports to:** Founder & Owner Bjørn Bahnsen

#### What you will build

You will build agents which can support businesses meeting their objectives on earnings and growth. Always having the existing organization in the center of the process.

**Our agents are at this point based on:**

- AI agent flows combining n8n for workflow orchestration and LangGraph / LangChain for the agent reasoning core.

- A clean API boundary between the workflow layer and the reasoning layer, so each part can evolve independently.

- Structured agent graphs for intent classification, entity extraction, product or order validation, tool calls, confidence scoring and response generation.

- Integrations to customer systems through MCP, REST APIs, SOAP, OData and database-backed adapters.

- Conversation memory, state checkpoints and audit trails using PostgreSQL.

- Queueing, caching and asynchronous jobs using Redis / BullMQ.

- PDF, report or certificate generation from HTML / CSS templates and headless rendering.

- Human-in-the-loop approval flows for order intake, sensitive decisions or low-confidence outputs.

- Observability, evaluation cases, logs and traces so Genta can trust, debug and improve every agent.


**Your responsibilities**

- Work with customers (from board level, CEO to employees in the different areas of the business), consultants and development partners to map workflows into buildable agent architectures.

- Translate flowcharts and business cases into technical specifications, delivery backlogs and production implementations.

- Build n8n automations, backend services, LangGraph nodes and integration adapters.

- Engineer prompts and structured outputs; design schemas, validation rules and error handling.

- Develop test cases, evals, fallback paths, retry logic and graceful degradation patterns.


- Package reusable components into Genta's agent toolkit so new customer flows can be built faster.

- Document what you build and train both internal colleagues, development partners and customer users.

- Keep the architecture practical, secure, maintainable and aligned with customer value and ROI.


#### **What you bring**

You are a strong match if you have built software that talks to messy real-world systems and you enjoy turning ambiguity into reliable workflows.

**AN ADVANTAGE TO HAVE**

- Strong backend or software engineering skills in TypeScript / Node.js and/or Python.

- Experience building APIs, integrations, automation workflows or event-driven systems.

- Practical experience with LLMs: tool calling, structured output, prompt design, guardrails and hallucination reduction.

- Ability to model business processes as state machines, graphs, queues or workflow systems.

- Comfortable with databases, especially PostgreSQL, plus version control, Docker and cloud deployment.

- A security and data-protection mindset: access control, auditability, GDPR, data minimisation and reliable logging.

- Excellent communication: you can explain technical decisions at eye level to non-technical customers.


**NICE TO HAVE**

- Hands-on experience with LangGraph, LangChain, LangSmith, n8n or MCP.

- Experience with Microsoft Dynamics NAV / Business Central, SOAP, OData or ERP integrations.

- Experience with Redis, BullMQ, Prisma, Puppeteer, email systems and SPF / DKIM / DMARC.

- Experience with AI evaluation frameworks, RAG, conversation memory or agent observability.

- Experience with ESG, CSRD, product data, document automation or compliance-heavy workflows.

- Danish language skills or experience working with Danish SMEs.


**What makes you a great fit**

- You are a builder, not a commentator. You like shipping systems that people actually use.

- You are curious and humble enough to understand the customer before writing code.

- You have strong product judgement: the simplest reliable solution is usually better than the most impressive demo.

- You care about trust, explainability and human-in-the-loop design.

- You can combine high-level architecture with concrete examples and working implementation.

- You get energy from creating reusable foundations, not just one-off scripts.


#### Why join Genta

Genta is positioned at the point where AI becomes operational. You will not be building gimmicks; you will build systems that save time, improve quality and help companies grow without losing control.

You will have influence over the technical foundation, the delivery model and how Genta explains AI to the market. Our style is practical, human and ambitious: no buzzwords without value, no black-box automation where accountability matters and no AI employees pretending to replace people. We build agents that support humans specifically and humanity in general and make companies and the world a better place to be.

You are welcome to apply even if you have not used every tool in the stack. We care about strong judgement, implementation ability and evidence that you can ship reliable systems. **But we care most about passion, curiosity and integrity.**
