## Scrive

Open positions (1)


Founded in Sweden in 2010, Scrive is a leading European provider of e-signature and identity solutions, trusted by 12,000+ customers across a wide range of industries.

Backed by Vitruvian Partners, we're scaling fast across the EU, with team members based across Europe and offices in Stockholm (HQ), Copenhagen, Berlin, Munich, Amsterdam, Brno, Oslo and Brno.

We offer the opportunity to be part of a company where your ideas matter and your contribution helps build the future of digital trust. 💫
