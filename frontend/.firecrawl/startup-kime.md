## KIME

Open positions (2)


KIME is redefining how brands grow in the age of AI.

We help companies become more visible across Large Language Models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. By analyzing how brands appear on these emerging platforms, KIME uncovers opportunities and delivers clear, actionable strategies to strengthen brand positioning, visibility, and reach.

Built for marketers, founders, and growth teams, KIME equips ambitious companies with the tools to stay ahead of the curve and gain a first-mover advantage in what we believe is the most significant shift in digital advertising in over a decade.

Our mission is simple: to ensure that when consumers ask AI, they find you.
