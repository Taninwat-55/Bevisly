#### About Teton

Our vision is to be the indispensable AI companion for care staff, transforming care, and ultimately operations and delivery across the entire healthcare and senior living industry,

Our product alleviates administrative inefficiencies and observational tasks that overburden healthcare professionals, and enabling them to provide better patient care.

Through a combination of in-room hardware, AI and desktop and mobile applications, Teton helps create a seamless healthcare experience for both patients and providers. Our solution provides unprecedented access to real-time data insights, creating a digital twin of patient states and care delivery that empowers healthcare providers with actionable intelligence for optimal decision-making.

#### Not Your Typical Job Description

Working in our Strategy & Operations team, you will operate across the full scope of the business, wherever support, structure, or execution is needed. If it happens in the company, you will likely play a part.

You will work as the right hand to our Director of Operations, helping to maintain and strengthen every part of the business as we grow. This is a core role for someone who wants to understand the full picture of how a company scales, and be trusted to help build it.

Your role will evolve with the pace of our growth, giving you exposure across all major parts of the business, in areas of finance, commercial operations, contracting, people operations, office operations, legal, corporate governance, supply chain and customer delivery, with some tasks on the lowest level of “getting shit done”, and other’s on providing insights to guide strategic direction.

#### **Key Responsibilities**

In practice, that means you’ll be:

- Working closely with the operations team on a range of strategic and operational priorities.

- Identifying operational challenges and proposing practical, data-driven solutions, as well as systemic system changes.

- Helping execute internal projects to improve processes, reporting, and operational efficiency across teams, and drive smarter decision-making.

- Jumping into whatever the week demands: a contract review before Friday, a customer escalation, a pricing model for a tender response, a forecast revision, a redline on a public-sector DPA, a quarter-end ARR reconciliation, or preparing analyses and business cases to advise leadership.


#### **Who we're looking for**

We are looking for people who believe in our long-term vision and value ownership and entrepreneurship rather than just another 9-5 job. With us you will have an opportunity to truly make an impact on the world with the outcomes of your work (and see the impact when visiting care facilities), while growing rapidly across several areas.

It matters less that you have an exact background, and more that you have the right mindset and capability to learn and grow, expanding your skills in systems thinking and AI.

Your profile could be:

- A bachelor's degree in International Business, Economics, Engineering, or a related analytical field (or at the end of your studies).

- You have strong analytical and communicational skills.

- You take ownership, solve problems independently, and move fast to get things done.

- You are curious about learning and using AI to drive business operations.


#### **What we offer**

- Equity in Teton (warrants)

- Work with state-of-the-art technology in a pioneering field.

- A role where you can make a significant impact on healthcare delivery

- Work with the smartest people, and opportunity to learn from your peers.


**For more information or questions please contact us at** [christian@teton.ai](mailto:christian@teton.ai)
