# JOIN THE CBSMUN ORGANIZATION

![](https://impro.usercontent.one/appid/oneComWsb/domain/cbsmun.dk/media/cbsmun.dk/onewebmedia/CBSMUN%202024/16-P1344411.jpg?etag=%22326437-671a9edb%22&sourceContentType=image%2Fjpeg&ignoreAspectRatio&resize=458%2B305&extract=0%2B23%2B458%2B282&quality=85)

There are many ways to get involved!

A conference like CBSMUN requires a large team and people of all different backgrounds and talents.

The organization is comprised primarily of CBS Students however it is open to outsiders as well\*

\*Please note certain roles require or prefer applicants who are CBS students, CPH-based and/or Danish speakers, if so it will be specified in the job description.

## APPLICATION CYCLES

All openings and application cycles are announced directly on our Instagram.

Note: Chair and Delegate applications operate differently and are done through MyMUN

## SECRETARIAT

The secretariat will be selected following each conference edition by the previous secretariat.

The Secretariat is the core team and the main organizers of the conference. The roles have high levels of responsibilities, while giving the chance to shape an international conference.

Interested applicants must send their CV incl. any MUN experience + a motivational letter and will go through an interview process.

Can not be combined with board membership of CBS UN

## TEAM MEMBERS

Team members are recruited in the beginning of the fall semester.

Team Members support the Secretariat within one of our six departments and help organize this conference in one domain. They help out all 4 days of the conference as well.

Can be combined with Chairing or Delegate in some cases. The delegate fee is waived for team members.

Interested Applicants must send their CV + a motivational letter.

## CONFERENCE VOLUNTEERS

Volunteers are taken on closer to the conference but the interest forms is open.

Volunteers help the conference run during the days of the event and assist with tasks on the ground.

Volunteers can sign up for different shifts and help at the conference on one or multiple days. All volunteers with multiple shifts may attend a social for free. Shifts during all 4 days are needed to get a citizenship point

No application necessary.
