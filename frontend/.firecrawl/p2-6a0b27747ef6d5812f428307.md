## Client Director

[Gneis Agency](https://thehub.io/startups/xlife-media) [København](https://thehub.io/jobs?location=Bredgade%2020A,%201260%20K%C3%B8benhavn,%20Denmark) [Full-time](https://thehub.io/jobs?positionTypes=5b8e46b3853f039706b6ea70)

Know an experienced client director or experienced digital consultant passionate about strategy and business development? We're looking for someone to join our close-knit team, make a real impact for clients, and help shape our agency's future.

* * *

**Must haves**

- Solid experience in digital strategy, consulting, and business development.

- Experience (7-10 years) from a similar role at an agency.

- Proven ability to manage client and project budgets to meet defined goals.

- Experience with change management and implementing new technologies.

- Skilled in new business initiatives and achieving sales targets.

- Fluent in both Danish and English (written and spoken).


**Nice to haves**

- Thrives on client dialogue and building strong, trusting relationships.

- A collaborative team player who contributes to shared success.

- Detail-oriented while maintaining a strategic overview.

- A relevant network and now how to capitalize on it.

- Creative, structured, and motivated by co-creating new ideas.

- Ambitious and curious by nature.


**About the job**

#### Shape the future of a growing agency.

As a Client Director, you will be a key sparring partner for our clients and a driving force in the agency's development. You will work at the intersection of strategy, marketing, brand, and sales, always with a holistic view and a focus on creating practical, effective solutions that make a real difference.

Develop and implement digital strategies for clients.

Provide strategic advice and facilitate client workshops.

Manage projects from idea to implementation, ensuring goals are met.

Identify business-critical areas for optimization with tech and/or AI.

Develop new client relationships, partnerships, and drive new business initiatives.

**What We Offer**

#### Join a team that values you

We believe in creating an environment where our team can thrive both professionally and personally.

**🚀 A Central Role**

Play a key part in our agency's growth and future direction.

**⚖️ Flexibility & Balance**

We offer the freedom and flexibility to balance your work and private life.

**🤝 Ambitious Team**

Become part of a strong, ambitious team that values diversity and different perspectives.

**💡 Tangible Impact**

Work closely with clients and see your work create concrete, measurable value.

**🌟 Partnership Opportunity**

For the right candidate, there is a clear path to a future partnership in the agency.
