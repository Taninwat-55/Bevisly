## Narrative Designer (Mobile Games)

[Cozy Game Studio](https://thehub.io/startups/cozy-game-studio) [Remote](https://thehub.io/jobs?countryCode=REMOTE) [Full-time](https://thehub.io/jobs?positionTypes=5b8e46b3853f039706b6ea70) [Freelance](https://thehub.io/jobs?positionTypes=5b8e46b3853f039706b6ea75)

Salary
€1,500 - €2,000

We are looking for a Narrative Designer/Writer for a 4-month contract (preferably freelance) starting in May. As our Narrative Designer, you will play a pivotal role in shaping the soul of _Spirit World_. You will collaborate closely with the team to flesh out a cozy magical world that feels alive and quirky.

The theme of the game is "becoming a witch," heavily featuring magic and nature, but we are stepping away from traditional medieval tropes. We need someone who can craft a world that feels fresh, modern, and inviting, and who can write dialogue that is cheeky, funny, and sharp.

#### **What you will be doing:**

- Fleshing out the world of _Spirit World_ collaboratively with the team

- Writing the overall story arc

- Creating new, memorable characters

- Designing story beats that fit seamlessly into our gameplay loops

- Writing dialogue


#### **Who this role is for:**

- You are a writer who thrives on cozy, magical, and nature-inspired themes.

- You have a knack for writing short punchy dialogue that pops.

- You want to use your narrative skills to promote wellness, relaxation, and self-care in players' lives.

- You actively support a culture of collaboration, mutual respect, and inclusiveness.


#### **Who this role is NOT for:**

- Writers looking to build dark, gritty, or medieval fantasy worlds. Our magic is cozy, modern, and quirky.

- Those who prefer to work in a silo; this role requires close collaboration to ensure the narrative coheres with the game design and art direction.


#### **Practicalities:**

- Position is contract-based - 4 months (full-time, part-time or freelance)

- Salary: 2000 EUR per month (full-time equivalent; pro-rated for part-time hours)\*

- The job is either remote or on-site at our office in V74, Vesterbrogade 74, Copenhagen V, Denmark

- Our working language is English


_\*We welcome applications from regions where this compensates well for a full-time role, but remain open to discussing a part-time arrangement pro-rated against this full-time equivalent budget._

#### **If you choose to work on site:**

- Free lunch

- Free coffee and tea in the office

- Heaps of love from our office dog Maki

- Social gatherings and events

- Inclusive culture

- Our working language is English


Join us at Cozy Game Studio and be part of a team dedicated to creating games that not only entertain but also promote wellness and relaxation in players' lives. Our company prides itself on fostering an inclusive and diverse workplace where creativity and innovation thrive. We strongly encourage applicants from all backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives to join our team. Being part of our company is not just about creating games; it's about contributing to projects that aim for the betterment of humanity. Here, you'll find an environment that values your unique contributions and supports your personal and professional growth. Join us in our exciting journey to make a positive impact on the world through the power of games.

Cozy Game Studio is a mobile game studio based in Copenhagen, Denmark. Our mission is to make self-care more accessible by integrating tools from positive psychology, meditation, and nervous system regulation into video games. We are currently working on our first title Spirit World - self-care mobile game about becoming a witch.
